About me

I am Alfred, who created this site as just my personal corner on the Internet. Over the past year, I have helped numerous students by tutoring them. During this process, I made quite a lot of teaching resources for them. Now that some of them have moved on to the next stages of their life, or that we have finished using the resources, I no longer find any use in them. This was my main motivation for creating this site.

Despite not being useful to me anymore, I still believe the resources are useful for anyone who has the passion and needs for a particular subject. So far, I have created resources in Math, English, Physics and Chemistry, all mainly following the HKDSE or UK A-Level curriculum.
