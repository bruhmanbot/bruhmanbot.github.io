Losing Tiles

In this section I will present the statistics I gathered for the losing tiles to see which tiles are the most common (and by how much).

The results

Occurences of different tiles as the losing/last tile:

As expected we see very little losses with lucky tiles as compared to normal tiles. While with the normal tiles, we see a concentration on tiles 2-7, with slightly fwer 8 tiles and nearly half as much 1/9 tiles as compared to the 2-7 tiles. I am unsure of what 8 tiles are not as common as the 2 tile. Mahjong tiles are symmetrical in nature, we should expect to see a symmetrical distrubution here. Although I have implemented randomisation to sometimes get the bot to favour the larger tiles, it seems as if this solution is insufficient to completely weed out the bias.

Contrary to my initial belief, the 3/7 tiles were not the most popular. My further analysis on this came to the following conclusion: since edge straights are not valued as much by the bots, this only leaves 1 possible open straight for the 3/7 tiles, (3 -> 4/5, and 7 -> 5/6) whereas the middle tiles (4/5/6) both have 2 possible open straights, leading to their high popularity.
